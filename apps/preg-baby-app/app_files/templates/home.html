Welcome to Pregnant Baby App

Pregnant Baby App helps you monitor your baby's development throughout pregnancy. Track fetal movements, follow weekly development updates, and keep a structured activity journal. The application provides insights into what is happening with both mother and baby at each stage of pregnancy.

You can use the kick counter to monitor daily activity, log baby movements in a journal, and receive alerts if decreased movement is detected. The weekly development guide explains key milestones and compares your baby's size to common fruits or objects for easier visualization.
